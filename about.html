About BDTV VOD

BDTV VOD is a browser‑based interface for browsing movies, TV shows, and BDIX IPTV streams in a clean, Netflix‑inspired dark layout.

Features

  • Trending, top‑rated, and now‑playing movie & TV sections powered by TMDB.
  • Integrated search across movies and series.
  • Per‑title player pages for movies and TV series via VidKing.
  • Live TV player with BDIX IPTV playlists and HLS support.
  • Mobile‑friendly responsive layout for phones and tablets.

Data & Streaming

Metadata (titles, posters, overviews, ratings) is provided by TMDB, while streams are hosted by third‑party providers (such as VidKing) and BDIX playlists. BDTV VOD does not host or store any video content.

Disclaimer

Streams are provided by external hosts and BDIX playlists. Availability and quality can change at any time. If a channel or VOD title does not play correctly, it is usually an issue with the external source rather than BDTV VOD itself.
