About MoonStream

MoonStream is a browser-based interface for browsing movies, TV shows, and BDIX IPTV streams in a clean, modern dark layout.

Features

TMDB-powered metadata
Trending, top-rated, and now-playing movie & TV sections.
Search movies & TV
Integrated search across movies and TV series.
BDIX Live TV with HLS
Live TV player with BDIX IPTV playlists and HLS.js support.
Mobile-friendly
Responsive layout optimised for phones and tablets.
Actor profiles
Browse actor pages with biography and filmography.
VidKing embeds
Per-title player pages for movies and TV via VidKing.

Data & Streaming

Metadata (titles, posters, overviews, ratings) is provided by TMDB, while streams are hosted by third-party providers (such as VidKing) and BDIX playlists. MoonStream does not host or store any video content.

Disclaimer

Streams are provided by external hosts and BDIX playlists. Availability and quality can change at any time. If a channel or VOD title does not play correctly, it is usually an issue with the external source rather than MoonStream itself.
